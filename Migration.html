The Journey of Hijrah

Prophet Muhammad (PBUH) and his followers migrated to Medina to escape persecution in Mecca. This journey marked the beginning of the Islamic calendar and the formation of the first Islamic state.

Impact of Migration

The migration united the Ansar (Helpers) and Muhajirun (Emigrants), laying the foundation for a just and inclusive society based on Islamic principles.
